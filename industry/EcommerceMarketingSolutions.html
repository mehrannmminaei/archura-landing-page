E-Commerce Marketing Questions

How quickly can we improve our store's performance?

+

Initial improvements typically appear within 30 days through quick wins like cart recovery campaigns, conversion optimization, and ad targeting adjustments. More substantial growth emerges over 3-6 months as we build stronger traffic sources, improve customer retention, and optimize the full purchase journey.

Which marketing channels work best for e-commerce?

+

The most effective mix varies based on your products and target customers. We typically see strong results from Google Shopping, social advertising, email marketing, and search optimization. Through testing and analysis, we identify which channels deliver the best return for your specific business.

How do you measure e-commerce marketing success?

+

We track the complete picture: traffic sources, conversion rates, average order value, customer acquisition cost, and return on ad spend. Most importantly, we focus on customer lifetime value to ensure we're acquiring shoppers who make repeat purchases, not just one-time buyers.

How do you balance marketplace and direct sales?

+

We develop a strategic approach that uses marketplaces for customer acquisition and expanded reach while building your direct channel for higher margins and customer relationships. This includes tactics to transition marketplace customers to your website for future purchases.

What makes your ecommerce marketing approach different?

+

We focus on the entire customer journey, not just top-of-funnel traffic. Our strategies address acquisition, conversion, and retention together, creating a complete system that grows sales while improving efficiency and return on your marketing investment.
