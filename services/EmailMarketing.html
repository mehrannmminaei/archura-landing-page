Our Email Marketing Process

We've refined this step-by-step approach with clients across industries to consistently deliver emails that engage and convert.

1

Audience Analysis

First, we examine your contact list to identify key segments, engagement patterns, and opportunities. For new programs, this includes developing smart contact acquisition strategies and list-building approaches.

2

Strategy Development

Next comes creating a complete email plan covering campaign types, frequency, content themes, and conversion goals. Each strategy aligns with your business objectives and customer lifecycle stages.

3

Campaign Creation

Our team develops compelling emails with attention-grabbing subject lines, engaging copy, and effective designs. The content focuses on driving specific actions while building long-term engagement.

4

Automation Setup

Building triggered email sequences that respond to customer behaviors comes next. These automated journeys deliver the right message at the right time without requiring manual sends.

5

Testing & Refinement

We systematically test subject lines, content approaches, send times, and design elements. Each campaign provides insights that strengthen future results.

6

Performance Tracking

Monitoring key metrics including open rates, click rates, conversion rates, and revenue is ongoing. Our reporting connects email activities to actual business outcomes to show the true impact.

Email Questions Answered

Everything you need to know about our email marketing approach and what makes it effective. We're transparent about our methods and results.

What results can we expect from email marketing?

+

Email consistently delivers one of the highest ROIs of any digital channel. Our clients typically see 20-40% improvements in engagement metrics within the first three months, with corresponding increases in conversion rates and revenue. Specific results depend on your current program maturity and industry benchmarks.

How do you handle personalization beyond just using names?

+

We implement behavioral targeting based on previous interactions, purchase history, and engagement patterns. This might include product recommendations, content tailored to specific interests, or unique offers based on customer value. Our segmentation strategies ensure messages reflect where each person is in their customer journey.

What makes your email approach different from other agencies?

+

We focus on driving actual business results rather than just pretty designs or standard templates. Our team combines marketing expertise with technical knowledge of advanced platform capabilities. Most importantly, we test everything and use real data for decisions rather than relying on industry "best practices" that may not work for your specific audience.

How quickly can you implement a new email strategy?

+

Basic campaigns can be up and running within 2-3 weeks. More complex automation programs typically take 4-6 weeks to build, test and launch. We prioritize quick wins while developing longer-term nurture sequences, giving you immediate results while building toward comprehensive coverage of the customer lifecycle.

Do we need to change our current email platform?

+

Not necessarily. We work with most major email platforms and can typically maximize your current technology. If your existing platform limits what you can achieve, we'll explain the specific limitations and suggest alternatives that might better serve your needs. Any platform recommendation comes with a clear cost-benefit analysis.
