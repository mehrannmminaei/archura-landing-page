Our PPC Process

A systematic approach that maximizes campaign performance from day one. We've refined this methodology across industries with consistent results.

1

Account Assessment

For existing accounts, we perform a complete audit identifying strengths, weaknesses, and opportunities. For new campaigns, we analyze your business goals, audience, and competitive landscape.

2

Strategic Planning

We develop a customized PPC strategy including platform selection, budget allocation, targeting parameters, and performance goals. Each strategy focuses on the specific metrics that drive your business.

3

Campaign Development

We build high-performance campaign structures with targeted ad groups, relevant keywords, compelling ad copy, and effective landing page strategies designed to maximize quality scores and conversion rates.

4

Launch & Initial Optimization

After launching campaigns, we monitor performance closely, making quick adjustments to bids, budgets, and targeting to ensure optimal performance from the earliest stages.

5

Performance Testing

We systematically test ad variations, landing pages, audiences, and bidding strategies to continuously improve campaign performance, focusing on the changes that drive the biggest impact.

6

Ongoing Management

Our team provides active campaign management, including regular performance reviews, competitive analysis, new opportunity identification, and strategic adjustments to maintain and improve results.

PPC Questions Answered

Clear information about how our PPC management works and what results you can expect. We believe in setting realistic expectations and explaining our approach.

How quickly will we see results from PPC?

+

PPC campaigns can generate traffic immediately after launch. However, optimization typically takes 2-4 weeks as we gather performance data and refine targeting. Most campaigns reach peak efficiency within 60-90 days as we test and improve all elements.

How do you measure PPC success?

+

We track platform metrics like clicks, impressions, and quality scores, but our main focus is on business outcomes: leads, sales, revenue, and return on ad spend. We connect campaigns to your actual business results using solid tracking and attribution.

What platforms do you work with?

+

We manage campaigns across Google Ads, Microsoft Ads, Facebook/Instagram, LinkedIn, TikTok, Twitter, and Amazon Advertising. We recommend platforms based on where your specific audience is most active and responsive.

What's your approach to budgeting?

+

We develop budgets based on your business goals and competitive landscape. Rather than fixed monthly spend, we focus on efficiency metrics like cost per acquisition and return on ad spend, scaling investment in channels that deliver the best results.

How do you handle restricted industries?

+

We have specialized experience navigating advertising restrictions in industries like iGaming, crypto and fintech. We develop compliant approaches that work within platform policies while still delivering strong performance.
