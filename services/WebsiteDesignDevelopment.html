How We Build Your Website

A straightforward development approach that delivers effective websites on time and focused on your specific business needs.

1

Discovery & Planning

We start by understanding what you want to achieve, who your customers are, and what your competitors are doing. This helps us set clear goals and technical requirements for the project.

2

Strategic Wireframing

Next, we map out your site structure, planning how users will navigate and where conversion opportunities should be placed. This creates the foundation before any design work begins.

3

Visual Design

Our designers create a custom look that matches your brand while focusing on user engagement. You'll see mockups of key pages and have the opportunity to provide feedback.

4

Development & Coding

Our development team builds your site with clean, efficient code that loads quickly and works well across all devices. We incorporate SEO-friendly structure and any custom features you need.

5

Testing & Refinement

Before launch, we test everything thoroughly across different devices and browsers. This quality check catches and fixes any issues with functionality, speed, or user experience.

6

Launch & Support

Once approved, we handle all technical aspects of going live and provide ongoing support. We're your partner in making sure your website continues to perform as your business grows.

Website Questions Answered

Practical information about our website design and development approach and what to expect when working with us.

How long does it take to build a new website?

+

Most business websites take 3-6 weeks from start to launch. Simple sites can be completed in as little as 2-3 weeks, while more complex platforms with custom features might take 6-8 weeks. We'll provide a specific timeline based on your needs during our initial consultation.

How do you make websites SEO-friendly?

+

SEO is built into our development process from the ground up. This includes creating clean code structure, proper heading hierarchy, fast page speeds, mobile optimization, schema markup, and user-friendly URLs. We also build sites with content management systems that make ongoing SEO maintenance easy for your team.

Will I be able to update the website myself?

+

Yes. We build sites on user-friendly content management systems that let you make common updates without technical knowledge. We provide training for your team and guides for specific site functions. For more complex changes, our support team is always available.

What if I need new features added later?

+

We design websites with future growth in mind. Our modular development approach makes it easy to add new features and functionality as your business evolves. Whether you need to add e-commerce capabilities, member portals, or custom tools, we can enhance your site without starting from scratch.

What makes your websites better for converting visitors?

+

We design with conversion in mind from the start. This means strategic placement of calls to action, creating visual hierarchies that guide attention, removing friction in conversion paths, and building trust elements throughout the site. Every design decision is made with your business goals as the priority.
