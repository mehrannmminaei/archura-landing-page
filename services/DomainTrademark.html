Our Brand Protection Process

A systematic approach to safeguarding your brand assets and market position.

1

Brand Audit

We start with a thorough inventory of your valuable brand assets including domains, trademarks, logos, and key terms. This creates the foundation for comprehensive protection.

2

Monitoring Setup

Our team implements automated scanning tools and alert systems across relevant channels. This ensures continuous protection without draining your internal resources.

3

Threat Detection

Regular monitoring identifies potential brand infringements and unauthorized uses. We spot domain squatters, counterfeiters, and trademark abuse before they impact your business.

4

Risk Evaluation

Each potential threat is analyzed for business impact and legal standing. This helps separate serious issues from minor concerns so you can allocate resources effectively.

5

Action Planning

For significant threats, we develop recommended response strategies. These range from simple takedown requests to more comprehensive legal approaches depending on severity.

6

Ongoing Protection

Continuous refinement of monitoring parameters as your brand evolves. This ensures your protection strategy grows with your business and adapts to new threat patterns.

Stay Ahead of Brand Threats

Brand Protection Essentials

Key information about protecting your brand and responding to potential threats.

How quickly can you identify new brand threats?

+

Most new infringements are detected within 24-48 hours of appearance. Our automated scanning runs continuously, with alerts triggered immediately when potential issues are found. High-risk threats receive priority notification to enable rapid response.

What types of brand infringements do you monitor?

+

We track domain variations, trademark usage, logo appropriation, counterfeit products, unauthorized resellers, and brand impersonation. Our monitoring also covers social media imposters, fake apps, and review manipulation that could damage your reputation.

How do you handle international brand protection?

+

Our monitoring systems cover global markets with localized expertise for major regions. We understand the different enforcement mechanisms across jurisdictions and can recommend appropriate actions based on where violations occur and your protection status in those markets.

What's the difference between monitoring and enforcement?

+

Monitoring identifies potential threats, while enforcement takes action against them. Our service focuses on comprehensive detection and documentation, providing the evidence your legal team needs to pursue effective enforcement when necessary.

Do you handle takedown requests and legal actions?

+

We provide all the documentation needed for takedown requests and legal proceedings. While we don't replace legal counsel, our reports and evidence collection make enforcement actions more efficient and effective when you decide to proceed.
