Privacy Policy for Archura Media
Effective Date: January 1, 2025

At Archura Media , accessible from https://archuramedia.com , your privacy is a priority. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your information when you interact with our website and services.

1. Information We Collect

a. Personal Information

When you contact us, request services, or sign up for updates, we may collect:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Company or business name
  • Billing and payment details
  • Other information voluntarily submitted by you

b. Automatically Collected Data

We may collect information about how you interact with our website, including:

  • IP address
  • Browser type and device
  • Pages visited and time spent
  • Referring URLs
  • Cookies and similar technologies

2. Services We Provide

We collect and use data to deliver our services, which include:

  • Search Engine Optimization
  • Conversion Rate Optimization
  • Consultation Services
  • PPC Advertising
  • Email Marketing
  • Website Design & Development
  • Landing Page Design
  • Analytics & Performance Reporting
  • Retention Marketing
  • Content Marketing
  • Domain & Trademark Monitoring
  • Grey Hat & Guerilla Marketing

3. How We Use Your Information

Your information may be used to:

  • Provide and maintain our services
  • Respond to inquiries and client requests
  • Process transactions and invoices
  • Send promotional and marketing content (if you opt-in)
  • Improve website performance and user experience
  • Comply with legal obligations

4. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share data with:

  • Trusted service providers (e.g., payment processors, analytics platforms)
  • Contractors or consultants working under confidentiality agreements
  • Law enforcement, if required by applicable law

5. Cookies & Tracking Technologies

We use cookies to:

  • Enhance functionality
  • Monitor website usage
  • Deliver personalized content

You can manage cookie preferences through your browser settings.

6. Data Retention

We retain your personal data only as long as necessary to:

  • Fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy
  • Comply with legal or contractual obligations

7. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to:

  • Access or correct your personal data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Object to or restrict processing
  • Withdraw consent at any time
  • File a complaint with a data protection authority

To exercise any of these rights, email us at admin@archuramedia.com .

8. Data Security

We use appropriate security measures (technical and organizational) to protect your data from unauthorized access, disclosure, or misuse.

9. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to third-party websites or services. We are not responsible for their privacy practices. We encourage you to read their policies before interacting.

10. Children’s Privacy

Our services are not intended for individuals under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with the revised “Effective Date.”
